Topline Paving and Landscapes

Paving, Landscaping & Fencing

At Topline Paving and Landscapes, we specialize in transforming outdoor spaces across Dublin with top-tier craftsmanship and attention to detail. Our comprehensive services cover everything from designing and installing driveways, patios, and pathways, to fencing, brickwork, and lawn care.

Whether you need durable surfaces with concrete and tarmac, lush greenery with turfing and artificial grass, or professional cleaning and sealing to maintain your property, our experienced team is dedicated to delivering exceptional results. Trust us to enhance your home's exterior with solutions that combine beauty, functionality, and lasting quality.

Contact Us

Services we offer

  • Driveways, Paths, and Block Paving

    Enhance your property's entrance and walkways with our expertly designed driveways, paths, and block paving solutions. We create stylish, durable surfaces that not only boost curb appeal but also provide practical and long-lasting functionality for any property.

  • Patios, Slabs and Natural Stone Products

    Enjoy outdoor living at its finest with our beautifully crafted patios and premium slabs and natural stone products. Whether you’re looking to relax, entertain, or simply elevate your outdoor space, our designs offer timeless beauty and durability tailored to your taste.

  • Fencing and Brickwork and Repairs

    Secure and beautify your property with our quality fencing and expert brickwork services. From new installations to meticulous repairs, we ensure that every detail contributes to a flawless and enduring finish that complements your home.

  • Turfing and Artificial Grass

    Achieve the perfect lawn with our professional turfing services or opt for a maintenance-free solution with our high-quality artificial grass. Whether natural or synthetic, we provide lush, green spaces that stay vibrant year-round, tailored to suit any garden.

  • Concrete and Tarmac

    For strong, versatile, and low-maintenance surfaces, our concrete and tarmac services are the ideal choice. Perfect for driveways, patios, and other outdoor areas, we deliver smooth, hard-wearing finishes that combine durability with aesthetic appeal.

  • Cleaning and Sealing

    Keep your outdoor spaces looking their best with our comprehensive cleaning and sealing services. We expertly maintain and protect driveways, patios, and roofs, ensuring they remain pristine, durable, and weather-resistant for years to come.
